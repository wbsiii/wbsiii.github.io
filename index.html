About me

"Quantitative social science" is the common thread between my research experiences and interests in social psychology, media effects, psychometrics, statistics, data science, user experience, interface design, and virtual reality. I'm fascinated by the challenge of psychological measurement, research methods, and inference. Sometimes it seems like modern social science is split between "numbers"-people and "people"-people, but I've always felt like both. With discipline, I've learned to accept my interdisciplinary nature.

When I'm not at a computer, I'm outside doing outside things (varies by season) and being affected by media (varies by mood).
